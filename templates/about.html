About Us

Who We Are

Welcome to Peace Mind Connect

Born out of a deep passion for mental well-being, Peace Mind Connect came to life in June 2024. We specialize in providing mental health and psychological services delivered by experienced, qualified, and verified professionals, including psychologists, therapists, and counselors. Our mission is to nurture the human mind, spirit, and body, helping individuals become the best version of themselves. Headquartered in Lagos, Nigeria, Peace Mind Connect is founded on four core principles: educate, connect, discuss, and research. We offer resources for everyone, from children and teens to adults, couples, and even organizations. Peace-Mind Connect allows you to access support from anywhere, anytime through our web app.

We collaborate with a growing network of verified mental health professionals who offer services across various platforms. These experts also contribute valuable insights and ideas on mental health and psychological well-being through their specialized writing. Our content is designed to be easily understandable without being overly vague or too clinical. To ensure efficient service delivery, we connect you with a verified mental health professional based on your unique conversation history with us. We also offer free resources to help you on your journey. Check them out [here].
